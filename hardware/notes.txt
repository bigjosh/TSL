1.
No ultrasound cleaning - RTC clock part U3 is sensitive to vibration.

2.
LCD parts L1 and L2 are to be hand soldered. They mount with the LCD face on the FRONT side of the PCB. 

3.
LCD parts L1 and L2 are heat sensitive. Avoid overheating when soldering.

4.
Use the solder reflow profile for RTC clock part U3, available on page 42 here...
https://support.epson.biz/td/api/doc_check.php?dl=app_RX8804CE&lang=en
Following this profile is critical for the performance of this part. 
All other parts are tolerant of this profile.
